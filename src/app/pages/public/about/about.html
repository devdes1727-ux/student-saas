About Us

Shaping artists, building confidence, and changing communities through art.

Sivaram Kalaikoodam is a premium art academy in Tirunelveli, founded to bring structured art education and social impact campaigns to the next generation.

Our Story

Since its inception, Sivaram Kalaikoodam has focused on creative excellence and community engagement. The academy blends discipline, mentorship, and real-world art initiatives that develop students both as artists and citizens.
